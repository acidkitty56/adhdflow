## Canva Setup Tips

1. New design → Custom size → 1000 × 1500px
2. Background: dark (#1a1a2e or similar dark navy) OR a screenshot of the app itself
3. Font: Inter Bold or Montserrat ExtraBold for the big text
4. Accent color: warm orange (#d97706 or similar — matches the app's code color)
5. Add the URL small at the bottom
6. Export as PNG
